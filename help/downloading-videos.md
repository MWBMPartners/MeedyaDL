## Downloading a Video

### Step-by-Step Process

> *Walkthrough of the video download workflow.*

1. Copy the video URL from Apple Music
2. Paste the URL into the gamdl-GUI URL input field
3. gamdl-GUI will detect that the URL points to video content
4. Select your preferred video quality and codec options
5. Click the download button
6. Monitor progress in the download queue

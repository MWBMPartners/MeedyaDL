## Your First Download

> *A quick walkthrough demonstrating how to download your first song or album.*

1. Open gamdl-GUI
2. Ensure your cookies are configured (see [Cookie Management](cookie-management.md))
3. Paste an Apple Music URL into the URL input field
4. Select your desired quality settings (see [Quality Settings](quality-settings.md))
5. Click the download button
6. Monitor progress in the download queue

For more details on downloading music, see [Downloading Music](downloading-music.md). For videos, see [Downloading Videos](downloading-videos.md).

# :musical_note: Downloading Music

This guide explains how to download songs, albums, and playlists from Apple Music using gamdl-GUI.

---

## Overview

gamdl-GUI supports downloading audio content from Apple Music by accepting URLs and processing them through the GAMDL backend. You can download individual songs, full albums, or entire playlists.
